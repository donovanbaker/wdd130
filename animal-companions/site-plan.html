Overview

Purpose

Have you ever you needed a little companion animal in these trying times? You may not know the type of companion you're looking for, or even where to look. I've built an informational site that will help you find an animal companion that's perfect for your needs. You'll learn about your companion's needs too, and what to expect from relationship with your chosen animal. My site will help people find healthy connection and partnership the world over.

Audience

The audience for this site is people of all ages and demographics. They will be from mostly middle class families, with a small amount of dispsable income. The audience will take pet ownership seriously, and want high quality, high value product recommendations. They will be working professionals looking for quick and easily digestible information.
